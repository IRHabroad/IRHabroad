What Is IRH

As a new and rapidly growing venture, 'Iranian Horizons Abroad' is dedicated to broadening the horizons for Iranian students. We aim to help young individuals realize their educational dreams by providing access to outstanding educational opportunities beyond the borders of Iran. Our company is actively seeking new and fruitful partnerships to establish long-term relationships and collectively expand the horizons for the youth of Iran. Join us on our exciting journey into the world of education."

  • At 'Iranian Horizons Abroad,' we offer a comprehensive package of scholarships, support, accommodation, and adaptation assistance to empower Iranian students to pursue their dreams of international education. Our commitment includes

    Scholarships: We provide financial aid and scholarships to deserving students, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their educational aspirations.
